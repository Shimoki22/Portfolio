About Me

ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Joaqui Joaquin, also known as Waki (sometimes Jackielyn, if you know me well). I'm a BSCS student who enjoys technology and creative projects. Even though I'm shy, I express my ideas confidently through schoolwork, designs, and other creative outputs.

Outside of academics, I enjoy K-pop (I'm a proud AROHA), cosplaying characters I love, photography, and casual dancing. I especially enjoy taking photos and being behind the camera, as it allows me to show my creativity and personality.

I believe creativity comes in many forms, and I enjoy turning simple ideas into something meaningful while growing and gaining confidence along the way.

My Learning Journey

I am a curious and motivated learner, always excited to explore new programming languages and technologies. I have a strong passion for discovery and enjoy developing my skills and knowledge in this course.

While I am particularly interested in UI/UX Design, I am also curious about Web Development, Data Analysis, Graphic Design, and other related fields.

Who I Am

Who I am and what drives me

I'm the type of person who observes first before speaking, but when it comes to projects and creativity, I go all in. I like making things that are simple yet meaningful, and I enjoy organizing my ideas so that people can easily understand them.
I stay calm under pressure at times, focus on what I do, and always try to give my best work. I love expressing myself through the things I create.

My Approach
  • Observe first, then act
  • Simple yet meaningful solutions
  • Express through creation
